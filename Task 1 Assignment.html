Program Events:

"Charity Run 2024" will feature an exciting lineup of events aimed at promoting health, wellness, and community involvement. The main objectives of this event are to raise awareness for various charitable causes and bring together the community for a meaningful day. The event will take place on October 11, 2024, at the Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC) in Ho Chi Minh City. Key activities include:

  • Opening Ceremony: Welcoming participants and introducing sponsors.
  • Charity Run: A 5 km run open to all ages and fitness levels.
  • Fundraising Auction: Featuring donated items from local businesses and individuals.
  • Live Performances: Entertainment from local artists.
  • Closing Ceremony: Announcing the top fundraisers and presenting awards.

Event Details:

The theme for this year's event is "Run for a Cause," emphasizing the importance of community involvement and support for charitable initiatives. The event aims to bring together people from all walks of life to contribute to a common goal of helping those in need.

Participation Information:

Participation in "Charity Run 2024" is open to runners, volunteers, and sponsors, each playing a crucial role in the success of this event. Here’s how you can get involved:

  • Runners:
    • Register online in advance or on the event day. The registration fee is $50, which includes a t-shirt, race bib, and refreshments.
    • Ensure to follow the race procedures and safety guidelines provided upon registration. All runners must be at the starting line 30 minutes before the race begins for a mandatory safety briefing.
    • Participants must be at least 16 years old and in good health. A health declaration form needs to be filled out during registration.
    • Detailed maps and route information will be provided at the registration booth and online.
  • Volunteers:
    • Help us make the event a success by volunteering in areas such as registration, race support, crowd management, and more. Sign up online to become a volunteer.
    • All volunteers will receive a training session prior to the event, including instructions on safety protocols and specific tasks.
    • Volunteers are expected to commit to a minimum of 4 hours on the event day.
    • Volunteers will receive a special t-shirt, a certificate of appreciation, and refreshments during their shift.
  • Sponsors:
    • Businesses can support by sponsoring a portion of the event or donating to the fundraising auction. Various sponsorship packages are available:
      • Bronze Package: $500 - Includes logo placement on event materials and social media shout-outs.
      • Silver Package: $1,000 - Includes all Bronze benefits plus a booth at the event and prominent logo placement.
      • Gold Package: $5,000 - Includes all Silver benefits plus naming rights to specific event sections and VIP access.
    • Exhibitors can rent a booth space for $200 to showcase their products or services.
    • Contributions to the fundraising auction are welcome. Donors will receive recognition in the event program and on the event website.
    • For sponsorship inquiries, please contact our sponsorship coordinator at sponsor@charityrun2024.org.

Clear Rules and Guidelines:

  • Safety First: All participants must adhere to safety guidelines, including wearing appropriate footwear, staying hydrated, and following race marshals' instructions.
  • Race Procedures: Detailed race procedures, including start times, route information, and finish line protocols, will be provided upon registration.
  • Code of Conduct: Participants are expected to maintain sportsmanlike behavior and respect all other participants and volunteers.
